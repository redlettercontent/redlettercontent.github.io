Sheryl Posnick is the founder and president of Red Letter Content. She has been in all corners of educational publishing, from writing math and reading curriculum, to planning and developing a yearly book list, to line editing career and test preparation books, to directing an editorial program. She has acted as the Book Program Director at LearningExpress, LLC, as an Editor at Kaplan Publishing, and as the Math Editor as part of Kaplan's curriculum team. Sheryl also has led creative writing workshops as part of the New York Writers' Coalition. She has a B.A. in English from Tufts University.
